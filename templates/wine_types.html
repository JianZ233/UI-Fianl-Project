What are the two basic types of wine?

Red Wine

Dark and Tart

Red wine, with its rich, velvety texture and complex flavors, offers a sensorial journey that captivates wine enthusiasts worldwide. Crafted from dark grape varieties like Cabernet Sauvignon and Merlot, red wine embodies a spectrum of tastes, from the robust notes of ripe berries and cherries to the subtle nuances of earthy undertones and spices.

White Wine

Light and Citrusy

White wine enchants with its crisp, refreshing character and vibrant fruit-forward flavors. Derived from green or yellow grapes like Chardonnay and Sauvignon Blanc, white wine presents a symphony of citrus notes, apple, and pear, complemented by delicate floral aromas.
