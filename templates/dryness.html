So what is the "dryness" of a wine?

Dryness refers to the level of sweetness of the wine.

Dry, least sweet

Most sweet

Higher acidity, the astringent taste of unripe fruit

More residual sugar/ripe fruit

The longer the fermentation, the lesser residual sugar, the dryer the wine gets.
